My Favorite Band: Tame Impala

My favorite band is Tame Impala, a psychedelic indie rock band from Australia. Formed in 2007, the band has seen several members come and go. The current lineup consists of:

  • Kevin Parker - Lead Singer
  • Dominic Simper - Guitar
  • Cam Avery - Bass
  • Jay Watson - Keyboard
  • Julien Barbagallo - Drums

For information about touring dates and news, visit their website.


Album Reviews

Innerspeaker, 2010
Tame Impala's first studio release introduced the world to their retro psychedelic sound. Recorded in a dilapidated house overlooking the Indian Ocean,
it has a distinctly less polished feel than later releases. Poplular tracks include Lucidity and Solitude is Bliss
Lonerism, 2012
Tame Impala's follow-up album has a much tighter theme than their first release, with lyrics and subjects focused on loneliness and isolation.
Musically, however, the album remains energetic and dynamic. Top tracks include Feels Like We Only Go Backwards and Elephant.
Currents, 2015
Tame Impala's best album to date features a more communal and upbeat sound than any of their other work so far.
This is an album that can be played on a summer drive or at a party with friends, and was their first album to reach #1 on the Australian charts. This album features my two favorite Tame Impala songs, The Less I Know The Better and Cause I'm a Man.
