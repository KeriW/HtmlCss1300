Pulp Fiction

My favorite film is Pulp Fiction, directed by Quinton Tarantino. The non-linear plot follows three interviewing stories: a pair of enforcers for a crime boss, a boxer on the run from the same crime boss, and a would-be Bonnie and Clyde.

  • John Travolta - Vincent Vega
  • Samuel L. Jackson - Jules Winnfield
  • Uma Thurman - Mia Wallace
  • Bruce Willis - Butch Coolidge
  • Ving Rhames - Marsellus Wallace
